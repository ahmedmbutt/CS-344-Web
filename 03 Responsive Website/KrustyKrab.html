It is a small Krabby Patty with a face that has small pickles for the eyes, ketchup for the pupils and a smile, and little antennas attached on it to make it look like a bug. It comes in the Krusty Kids Meal.

It is a bacteria-infected Krabby Patty that is very poisonous. It is covered with green mold and hairs, and contains tainted buns, meat, and cheese.
